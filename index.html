ClearField
Gene Ball
June 2023

Clear the field of mines by clicking on non-mined cells and marking cells that contain mines. Cleared cells (usually) contain a digit indicating the number of mines in the 8 adjacent cells. Every initial configuration can be cleared without guessing. Clicking a cell clears it, and long-clicking (or right-clicking) a cell marks it as a mine (long-clicking a marked cell again clears it).

Clearing a mine counts as an error. If you get stuck, Hint will highlight a cell that contains enough information to correctly clear or mark adjacent cells. A game is completed successfully if the entire field is marked correctly with fewer than 3 errors (and fewer than 3 hints).

Choose a game at one of 4 levels of difficulty, or play the Game of the Day.
Stats for each difficulty level include:

  • the number of games you have completed,
  • the number successfully completed in a streak,
  • the number of games completed perfectly (no errors or hints),
  • the length of your streak of perfect games.
You will be informed if you are the first player to perfectly complete a particular game, or you complete it faster than anyone else. User Records keeps track of the number of 'first games' you have achieved, and the number of fastest times that you still hold.
